How It Works

1

Encode

Both images are passed through a pre-trained VGG‑19 encoder to extract deep feature representations.

2

Align

AdaIN aligns the content features' mean and variance to match those of the style features in real time.

3

Decode

A trained decoder network reconstructs the final image from the stylized feature space back into pixels.
